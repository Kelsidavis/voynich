## Phonetic Analysis: "osaiin" = "osatu"

| Feature | Basque "osatu" | Voynich "osaiin" |
|---------|----------------|------------------|
| Initial | os- | os- |
| Root | osa- | osa- |
| Suffix | -tu (verb) | -iin (Voynich suffix) |

The change from `-tu` to `-iin` is consistent with:
- Voynich grammatical suffixes (-aiin, -iin, -ain common)
- Sister language different verb morphology
- Same semantic root, different grammatical structure
