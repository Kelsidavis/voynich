## Implications

### For Sister Language Theory

Number systems can diverge significantly between related languages:
- Basque numbers are very distinct (vigesimal base-20 system)
- A sister language might have developed completely different numbers
- This doesn't necessarily refute the Pre-IE hypothesis

### For Manuscript Analysis

The complete absence of recognizable numbers suggests:
1. Numbers may be written symbolically (not as words)
2. The text may not require numerical expression
3. Or the "language" may not be natural language

### Recommendation

Examine the manuscript for:
- Numerical symbols or glyphs
- Tally marks or counting systems
- Any patterns that might represent quantities
