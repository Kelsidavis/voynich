   - Rivers (four rivers of Eden?)

3. THE LABELS contain:
   - Celestial terms (otar, otal, otedy)
   - Medical instructions (dar, dal, chor)

4. THE PURPOSE:

   - "When star X is in position, give treatment Y to patient Z"
