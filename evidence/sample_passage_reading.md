### The Fundamental Problem

- **Identified**: ~20% of unique words
- **Unknown**: ~80% of unique words

**The manuscript cannot be "read" with current knowledge.**

1. Most vocabulary remains unknown
2. Grammar is unique to Voynich
3. Function words are absent
4. No bilingual text exists for verification

## What This Tells Us

### Supports Sister Language Theory

The pattern we see is consistent with a **sister language to Basque**:
- Some shared vocabulary (content words)
- Completely different grammar
- Many words with no Basque cognate

### Does NOT Support

- Voynich = Basque in code (grammar too different)
- Voynich = pure cipher (vocabulary matches too specific)
- Random/meaningless text (too structured)
